The Ash Grey Liberty Heathers Carpet has a mid-grey look with a mix of grey fibres in each tuft.

The twist pile is made using a 2-ply process. This is where two different colours are woven together in each tuft. This is quite a popular look as it can help reduce the appearance of foot-marks and dirt.

Made of stain resistant polypropylene, this carpet is made using modern materials and processes. You can easily clean those everyday spills and dirt with bleach with no worries about the colour! As well as this, it’s very hard wearing, but still soft and comfortable underfoot!
